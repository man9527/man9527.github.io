Chi-Ming Tseng and Shu-Yun Sun Donation of Insulated Comforters to TSA
Chi-Ming Tseng, division scientist at 3M, and his wife, Shu-Yun Sun, both long time members of TAAMN, have since 2002 donated more than 100 insulated comforters to students of Taiwan Student Association at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities. The Tsengs have been living in the Twin Cities for more than 19 years and made great contributions to the local Taiwanese community. At the 2005 Lunar New Year Party, Susan Sun, president of TSA, presented two official TSA T-shirts to the Tsengs as a token of appreciation for their generosity.
