228 Memorial Concert: "Love and Hope," February 26, 2005
The Formosa Chorus of TAAMN presented a memorial concert at the University of Minnesota St. Paul Student Center Theater on February 26, 2005 to commemorate the 228 Incident that took place in 1947, in which hundreds of thousands of Taiwanese were killed by the military force of the Nationalist government. It took nearly 50 years for the government to formally apologize to the families of the victims and to compensate for their suffernings and humiliations. February 28 has been designated by the government as Peace Memorial Day. The Taipei New Park was renamed 228 Memorial Park.
