Cook for Kids at the Ronald McDonald House, February 8, 2005
Nineteen members from TAAMN and TSA went to the Ronald McDonald House near the University of Minnesota Medical Center on Tuesday, February 8, 2005, to serve a Lunar New Year's Eve dinner for all residents at the House, including the Chang family. This is part of the RHM's Cook for Kids program. Led by Jenn-Feng Yan, president of TAAMN, and Susan Sun, president of TSA, the group of volunteers prepared several delicious courses and performed some light entertainment, such as lion dance, Taiwanese yo-yo and traditional Taiwanese music. Yan also handed out "red envelopes" to children at the House.
