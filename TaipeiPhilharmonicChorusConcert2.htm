TAAMN Dinner Reception for the Taipei Philharmonic Chorus
TAAMN hosted a dinner reception for the Taipei Philharmonic Chorus on Sunday, August 4, 2002 at the Ho Ho Restaurant. At the reception were Shane T. Wu, director of Cultural Division of the Taipei Economic & Cultural Office in Chicago, his wife and Lawrence Hsiao-Feng Tseng, senior assistant to Director General of TECO.
