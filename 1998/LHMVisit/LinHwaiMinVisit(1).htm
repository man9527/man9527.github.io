Cloud Gate Dance Theatre Director Lin Hwai-Min Visit

Lin (fourth from left) with TAAMN representatives
Artistic Director Lin Hwai-Min and 24 members of the Cloud Gate Dance Theatre of Taiwan presented a fabulous show in the Northrop Auditorium at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities on February 25, 1998. Following the performance, Juey-Hong Lai (third from left), president of the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter, and Wen Lin, Liyun Tsong, Edwin Hsu, Joanna Hsu, Jeff Chang, Chi-Ming Tseng, Yeou-Ren Tsai, Kemi Tsai, and Chung-I Young welcomed Lin at a recpetion co-hosted by TAAMN.
