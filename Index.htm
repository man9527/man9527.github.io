228 Memorial Concert


The Formosa Chorus of TAAMN presented a memorial concert at the University of Minnesota St. Paul Student Center Theater on February 26, 2005 to commemorate the 228 Incident that took place in 1947, in which hundreds of thousands of Taiwanese were killed by the military force of the Nationalist government. The 20 songs included Taiwanese, Chinese and aboriginal folklores. More than 100 people attended the concert and the reception. More here.
