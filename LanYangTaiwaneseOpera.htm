Seeking Herb to Save Husband
This is an act from a famous traditional drama, Legend of the White Snake. The White Snake, Beh Soh-Chin, drank realgar wine during the Dragon Boat Festival. The effect of the realgar forced her to expose her true form, a white snake, which shocked her husband into unconsciousness. To save her husband from dying, she went to the God of Longevity's sacred mountain to seek a legendary herb. However, the god disapproved of the marriage between a snake and a man. Would the god let her leave safely? Would she be able to save her husband?

Taiwanese Music Concert Deities Celebrating New Year
Lan-Yang Company orchestra is the first of its kind in the Taiwanese history to have attributed a whole concert to the traditional music in the prestigious Taiwan National Concert Hall in Taipei and the Taipei Social Education Hall. For the performance in the Twin Cities, the orchestra has carefully strung and compiled Taiwanese folk songs, popular songs and music, including "Expecting the Spring Breeze," "Jasmine Flowers in June," and Tohuei Crossing the River." The band also performed such American folk songs as "Yankee Doodles." On the New Year Day of 2002, Mah Tsoh, the Holy Mother in Heaven, suddenly saw an auspicious light shining brightly in Taiwan area. She sent her messengers, Chen Li Gan and Sun Hong Nih, to invite other deities to go to Taiwan to help celebrate. Deities of Happiness, Wealth and Longevity as well as the famous Eight Deities of Hong Lai all went along, bearing blessings and nice gifts. The whole Taiwan area was filled with heavenly music and auspicious aura. Deities and mortals celebrated New Year together happily. (canceled)
Beating Deity Seng Hong
In order to avoid being drafted by the emperor, three civilians fled home and hid in a Seng Hong temple. To their great dismay, the drafting officers also came to the temple to wait out the heavy snow. In face of this dire situation, they decided to dress up as the deity Seng Hong, his judge and the imp to fool the officers. Will this work? Can they be spared?
