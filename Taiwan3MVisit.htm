Taiwan 3M Delegation Visit
A 19-member delegation of Taiwan 3M managers, agents and their families toured Minnesota and 3M headquarters in the Twin Cities during October 8-14, 2001. John Ko, vice president of TAAMN, hosted a reception for them. Point at the picture to see TAAMN members at the welcome party.
