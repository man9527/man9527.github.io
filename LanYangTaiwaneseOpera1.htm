Lan-Yang Taiwanese Opera Company Visit
Manager Chiu-Chin Tien (left in the second picture), first lady of the I-Lan County, Taiwan, and performers of the 38-member Lan-Yang Taiwanese Opera Company met with the audience at the reception following the program. Attended by about 260 people, the show lasted for more than two hours. The Twin Cities performance was part of Lan-Yang's month-long 2002 North America Tour. It was presented by the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter and the Taiwanese Foundation of Minnesota. More photos here.
