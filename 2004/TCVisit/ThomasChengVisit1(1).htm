TECO/Chicago Director-General Thomas Cheng Visit, March 3, 2004
Thomas Cheng (first row, sixth from left), director-general of Taipei Economic and Cultural Office (TECO) in Chicago, hosted a dinner reception on March 3, 2004 for officials and legislative representatives of the State of Minnesota. Attending the reception were Mary Kiffmeyer, secretary of state; Mee Moua, senate majority whip; Matt Kramer, commissioner of Department of Employment and Economic Development; Tony Lorusso, director of Minnedota Trade Office; Lyndon R. Carlson, state representative; Linda Berglin, state senator; and Sandra Pappas, state senator. TECO officials included Deputy Director-General Paul Wang, Director Kuei-Jung Huang, Director Peng-Kuen Chiang, Director Shane Wu, Senior Assistant Paul Chen. and Senior Assistant Sophie Chou.
