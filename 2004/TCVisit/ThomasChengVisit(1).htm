TECO/Chicago Director-General Thomas Cheng Visit, March 5, 2004
Thomas Cheng, director-general of Taipei Economic and Cultural Office (TECO) in Chicago, and other officials visited the Twin Cities between March 3 and 5, 2004. He hosted a dinner reception for representatives and individuals from various Taiwan-related organizations, including TAAMN and University of Minnesota Taiwan Student Association. TECO officials present at the reception were Deputy Director-General Paul Wang, Director Kuei-Jung Huang, Director Peng-Kuen Chiang, Director Vicky Chuang, Deputy Director Mike Lin, and Senior Assistant Paul Chen. Director Shane Wu and Senior Assistant Sophie Chou left before the dinner. More here.
