TECO/Chicago Poa-Shing Wang and Vicky Chuang Visit
At the invitation of TAAMN, Poa-Shing Wang (front, second from left), deputy director general, Taipei Economic and Cultural Office in Chicago, and Vicky Chuang (first from left) of the Chinese Culture Center of TECO, attended a celebration for the inauguration of President Chen Shui-Bian at the U of M Coffman Union on May 21, 2004. Jenn-Feng Yan (second from right), vice-president of TAAMN presided over the celebration. TAAMN also held a photo exhibition of Taiwanese cultures and natural beauty. Wang and Chuang later attended the Taiwanese Hakka Chorus performance on the same day. Read the related stories in the World Journal below and here.
