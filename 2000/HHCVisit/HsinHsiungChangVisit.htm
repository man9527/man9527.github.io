Science Division Director Hsin Hsiung Chang Visit

Visitors from Taiwan
Hsin Hsiung Chang (front row, third from left), director of Science Division of the Taipei Economic & Cultural Office in Chicago, visited the Twin Cities on September 19, 2000. He met with Juey-Hong Lai, David Lin (front row, first two from left) and Tsan-Kuo Chang of the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter and other representatives of Taiwan-related organizations.
