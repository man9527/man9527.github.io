  活動內容:
 
 我們很榮幸邀請到劉孟隴 ( Dr. Andrew Liu )牙科醫師來演講  
"成人常見的口腔問題"。劉醫師是位資歷淵博的青年才俊、
他更是位和藹可親充滿熱心的台灣同鄉、目前在 Vally Dental Group 
服務。這次演講機會極難得、而口腔衛生保健人人皆有切身的需要、
因此我們特別將這例會對外公開給所有的同鄉受益 (不帶小孩)、
同時歡迎新會員加入。
 
  
此次活動完全免費、但一定要在7月4日星期二以前報名、因準備教材
茶點須預知人數、而且秀芳美麗的花園後院雖然寬敞但地方有限。
會後自費自由參加餐館晚餐交誼。
 
  MTWA   吳朱實會長、陳秀芳副會長  謹上
 
Andrew M. Liu, D.D.S.
- General Dentist
 
 Dr. Liu was born in Taiwan and moved to New York at the age of 15. In 1993 
he began attending the State University of New York at Buffalo, where he 
subsequently received both his Bachelor of Art in Biology and Economics and 
Doctor of Dental Surgery Degrees.
 
 Following graduation, Dr. Liu and his wife relocated to the Twin Cities. He continued 
his dental education at the University of Minnesota by completing a postgraduate 
residency of Advanced Education in General Dentistry in 2004. In September 2004, 
Dr. Liu became a full time associate with Valley Dental Group and practices all facets 
of General Dentistry.
 
In his free time, Dr. Liu is actively involved with his charity group as well as many
volunteer works in the community. He and his wife also enjoy many activities that the 
great state of Minnesota has to offer.
