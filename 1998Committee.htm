TAAMN 1998 Executive Committee

The Executive Committee consists of the following positions. Except for the president and vice-president, which are elected by the general assembly of TAAMN at the annual lunar new year celebration, the other positions are appointed by the president. These members serve a one-year term and can be reappointed.
President: Juey-Hong Lai Program: Ying-Yuh Lu
Vice-President: Edwin Hsu General Administration: Hsing-Kuen Liou
Treasurer: Hilda Huang Newsletter Editor: Ling Yang
