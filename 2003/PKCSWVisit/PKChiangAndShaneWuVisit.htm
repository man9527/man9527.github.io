TECO Peng-Kuen Chiang, Director of Science Division, and Shane T. Wu, Director of Cultural Division, Visit
Peng-Kuen Chiang, director of Science Division of Taipei Economic and Cultural Office (TECO) in Chicago, and Shane T. Wu, director of Cultural Division of TECO, visited the Twin Cities May 8-9, 2003. They were accompanied by Richard Chang of Science Division. On May 8, they toured Mayo Clinic in Rochester. The next day, they met with Professors Wei-Shou Hu, David Du, Wei-Chung Hsu, Cheng-Cher Huang, and Ching-Chang Ko of the University of Minnesota and toured their labs. They held a luncheon for Dean H. Ted Davis and Associate Dean Peter Hudleston, both of the Institute of Technology, as well as Joseph R. Allen, chair of Asian Languages and Literatures, and 8 other faculty members of the University. They also toured Medtronic hosted by Ms. Hsin-Huey Lin, senior design automation engineer. They had dinner with 25 members and friends of the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter.
