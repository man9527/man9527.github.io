Spring once again comes to Taiwan. Fragrant flowers and burgeoning trees cover all the mountains across the beautiful island.

Butterflies are flying. Bees are buzzing. Young men and women are getting together to celebrate the return of spring.

Their romantic encounters create a love story that has been passed down from generation to generation in Taiwan.
