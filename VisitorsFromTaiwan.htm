xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx January 16, 2006
green
Many individuals and groups, both official and civilian, from Taiwan have visited Minnesota since the 1970s. They came to share with the Taiwanese community in the Twin Cities their views, experiences, aspirations, and struggles concerning every aspect of life in Taiwan, including domestic politics, economy, education, labor, dance, music, health, journalism, literature, and foreign relations. TAAMN has either been the local host or joined other Taiwan-related organizations to extend its warm welcome to these individuals and groups.x
