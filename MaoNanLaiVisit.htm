OSSSC Director Mao-Nan Lai Visit, September 1, 2000
Mao-Nan Lai (center, front row), director of Overseas Scholars & Students Service Center, National Youth Commission, Executive Yuan, Taiwan, visited the Twin Cities on September 1, 2000. He met with 18 representatives from Taiwan-related organizations, including Ching-Chang Ko, president of the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter, and TAAMN members Juey-Hong Lai and Tsan-Kuo Chang.
