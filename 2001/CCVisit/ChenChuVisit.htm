Council of Labor Affairs Chairperson Chen Chu Visit, July 23, 2001
Chen Chu, chairperson of the Council of Labor Affairs, Executive Yuan, Taiwan, visited the Twin Cities on July 23, 2001. She spoke to about 40 TAAMN members on a variety of topics, including the current labor environment in Taiwan, at a reception hosted by Ying-Yuh Lu, president of TAAMN, at the Ho Ho Restaurant. This was Chen's fourth visit to Minnesota. Many members have known her for years.
