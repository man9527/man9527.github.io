TECRO Deputy Representative Ying-Yuan Lee Visit

A member of TAAMN in the early 1990s, Ying-Yuan Lee (right), deputy representative of Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office in the United States (TECRO), visited the Twin Cities on July 8, 2001. He met with Minnesota Secretary of State Mary Kiffmeyer and Vincent Wang, president of Formosan Association for Public Affairs-Minnesota Chapter at a reception hosted by TAAMN. Lee returned to Taiwan to become chief of staff in the Executive Yuan in January 2002. He later ran for mayor of Taipei, capital of Taiwan, but lost to the incumbent.
