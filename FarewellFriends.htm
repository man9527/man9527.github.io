TAAMN Remembers

Since the late 1990s, because of job changes or retirement, many longtime TAAMN members have moved out of Minnesota. During their sojourn in the Twin Cities, these members and their families had served TAAMN well, helping to lay a solid foundation for the Taiwanese community to grow. This page is dedicated to their contribution and services to promote awareness and understanding of Taiwan and the Taiwanese culture in Minnesota.
