This is the only known cover of the 1967 Directory of Formosan Society in Minnesota that has survived over the years. Yeou-Ren Tsai's dedication helps preserve the collective memory of the Taiwanese community in the Twin Cities. As written on the cover, there were 114 members.

The Executive Committee of the Formosan Society in Minnesota, the predecessor of the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter, consists of president, vice-president and coordinators of divisions, each of which includes several subcommittee members. The Society has 20 advisors.

TAAMN thanks the pioneers who had the vision to see the importance of the Taiwan identity in the State of Minnesota. Their foresight, aspirations and love for Taiwan were commendable and remarkable in the early years as they strove to forge a sense of being Taiwanese when it was most difficult for them to do so. Below are the pioneers who had paved the way for many more Taiwanese to follow in the years to come. Those who still live in Minnesota are highlighted.
