TECO/Chicago Director-General Antonio C. S. Chen Visit
Antonio C. S. Chen, director-general of Taipei Economic and Cultural Office (TECO) in Chicago, visited the Twin Cities on February 19, 2002. He hosted a reception for 29 representatives from various Taiwan-related organizations, including Shr-Wei Chen (left), president of University of Minnesota Taiwan Student Association and John Ko, Chi-Ming Tseng, Ching-Chang Ko, and Tsan-Kuo Chang of the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter as well as Vincent Wang (right) of Formosan Association for Public Affairs.
