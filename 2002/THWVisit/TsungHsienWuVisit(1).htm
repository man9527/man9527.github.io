TECO Cultural Division Director Shane T. Wu Visit

Shane T. Wu (second row, second from left), director of Cultural Division of the Taipei Economic & Cultural Office in Chicago, and his wife visited the Twin Cities on August 4 and 5, 2002. He met with members of the Taipei Philharmonic Chorus from Taiwan at a dinner reception hosted by the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter (see below) on August 4. The next day, he hosted a dinner for Li-Jen Peng, Shr-Wei Chen, Yi-Hung Tseng, Shu-Chen Huang, Yu-Ying Liao, Feng-Yi Wang, Jo-Anne Chiang, and Fu-Hsiung Tsay of Taiwan Student Association and John Ko of TAAMN.
