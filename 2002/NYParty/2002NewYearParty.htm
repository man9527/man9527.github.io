Special Thanks to James Wu for His Generous Donation


 James Wu, a lifetime TAAMN member, has again donated $1,000 in the form of "red envelopes" for the new year celebration. Since his by-pass surgery in January 2001, Wu has been feeling better and stronger. Last September, he joined an international group to visit Europe for 17 days. They went to England, Belgium, Luxembourg, Austria, Hungary, Slovakia, Czeck Republic and Germany. Among the 39 people, Wu said he was the second oldest.
