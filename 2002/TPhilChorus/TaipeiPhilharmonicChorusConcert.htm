Taipei Philharmonic Chorus Visit
The Taipei Philharmonic Chorus performed on Sunday, August 4, 2002 at the Minnesota Hisotry Center. This public concert was part of the International Choral Festival, co-sponsored by the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter and the Minnesota Historical Society. About 350 people attended the concert at the 315-seat 3M Auditorium. Additional seats were added to accommodate more people. The performance was well received by the full house audience.
