TAAMN Donated 2,224 3M N95 Respirators to the "Anti-SARS Foundation" in Taiwan
Members of TAAMN executive committee met May 18, 2003 at the residence of John Ko (fourth from left), president of TAAMN, to donate 2,224 3M N95 respirators to the Anti-SARS Foundation in Taiwan. Present at the meeting were Tsan-Kuo Chang, Shr-Wei Chen, Kuang-Sheng Cheng, Steve Fan, Chih-Chung Hsu, Sheau-Ping Hu, Cheng-Cher Huang, Chung-Linn Kao, John Ko, Mei-You Ko, Juey-Hong Lai, Li-Huey Lai, Chi-Ming Tseng, Vincent Wang, Pei-Wen Wu, Jennfeng Yan, and Edward Yu. 3M Company helped ship the masks to 3M Taiwan. Special thanks to Attila Erdos, Double Yeh-Liu, Nancy Matthews, Mike Mulligan, Dan George, John Swenson, Greg Nelson, Jeff Brathal, and Leaan Jansen for their assistance.
