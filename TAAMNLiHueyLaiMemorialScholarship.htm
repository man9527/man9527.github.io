TAAMN Li-Huey Lai Memorial Scholarship
For the first time since its inception, TAAMN has established a memorial scholarship to commemorate the life of a long time member. Mrs. Li-Huey Lai, a compassionate woman, had devoted her life to promote Taiwan and its culture in Minnesota. Li-Huey died on September 8, 2004 during cancer surgery in Rochester. Her story was reported in a lengthy obituary in the Star Tribune on September 18, 2004. Members of the TAAMN executive committee met on May 1, 2005 to discuss a proposal to establish a memorial scholarship in memory of her life and her dedication to the Taiwanese American community in the Twin Cities. By a unanimous vote, the scholarship was established, effective immediately. Read the English version or download the application form.
