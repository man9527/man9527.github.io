Taiwanese Pop Singer Hong Rong-Hong Visit, July 15, 1999
At the invitation of the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter and the Evangelical Formosan Church of the Twin Cities, Hong Rong-Hong, a Taiwanese pop singer and winner of numerous musical awards in Taiwan, and his wife (right) visited the Twin Cities on July 15, 1999. They performed a number of touching popular Taiwanese songs and hymns at the Formosan Church.
