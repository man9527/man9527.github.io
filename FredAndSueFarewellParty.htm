Farewell Party: Fred Juang and Sue Lin
October 25, 2002

Pioneers of TAAMN, Fred Juang and his wife, Sue Lin, moved to Diamond Bar, California in November 2002 to be closer to their daughters. But "not too close to be comfortable," said Fred. Fred and Sue had lived more than 35 years in New Brighton and have made significant contributions to the Taiwanese community in the Twin Cities. About 40 TAAMN members gathered at the Peking Garden restaurant to bid them farewell. They will be greatly missed. 		Point at any picture to see more.
