Farewell Party: David Lin and SuYuan Wu
May 25, 2002

After living more than 20 years in New Brighton, David Lin and his wife, SuYuan Wu, relocated to Phoenix, Arizona in June 2002. Both long time members of TAAMN, David and SuYuan have made significant contributions to the Taiwanese community in the Twin Cities. Thirty seven TAAMN members gathered at the Peking Garden restaurant to bid them farewell. Later, the group went to Juey-Hong Lai and Li-Huey Chang's residence to share fond memories with them. They will be greatly missed. 		Point at any picture to see more.
