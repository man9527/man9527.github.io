News From Taiwanese Association of America
May 24-May 27, 2002 The first TAA Biennial Convention was held at the Washington Hilton in Washington, DC. Former President Lee Teng-hui was scheduled to give a keynote speech on May 25, but later canceled plans to visit the United States. The Lan Yang Taiwanese Opera Company performed on Sunday, May 26.
February 2, 2002 The TAA launched an impressive campaign for the 9-11 Relief Fund nationwide through the TAA grassroots network, raising approximately $187,000. Among this amount, $24,000 has been donated to the victim families of the Pentagon tragedy. The remaining fund, $162,966.65, which includes $6,605 contributed by San Jose Canaan Taiwanese Christian Church, will be donated to the families of New York City policemen and fire fighters who died in the wake of terrorist attack on the World Trade Center.
