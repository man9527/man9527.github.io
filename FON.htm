Since 1979, the Taiwanese Association of America-Minnesota Chapter has represented Taiwan and its culture at the annual Festival of Nations sponsored by the International Institute of Minnesota. In 1987, TAAMN's cultural exhibit won the first award of excellence at the inaugural cultural exhibits competition. Since then, the Taiwanese cultural exhibits have won numerous awards of excellence.
